Video Game Sales

18th of May 2022

Introduction

With the rise of video games, nothing has been more indicative and representative of an ever changing market. Highlighting technology's rises and crashes of trends as wellas the technological priorities as set by major technology companies.

In this analysis I shall analyse video game sales ever since the inception of PONG to prior to 2020.

Methodology and Technology

In terms of programming I shall be utilising Python 3.10.X with the following libraries:

  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Seaborn
  • Scikit-learn

I shall be writing my scripts and displaying my work/visualisations in a JupyterNotebook.
