PC Parts List

8th of May 2022

While not recently, I have indeed upgraded my PC since graduation from High School and beginning University. This is also the first PC that I will personally own as the PC that I have interacted with in a non-school/library setting was my home PC that ran Windows XP. I assume that people who stumble upon this blog might be curious about the hardware that I use to run MS Paint write amazing scripts for data analysis and visualisation. ;)

With the assistance from my wife, I was able to name the 'beast', "Evelyn". Stylised with ALL CAPSof course.

So far, I can say that my experience with the PC is a rather positive experience. I am finally able to play TF2 without the fear of the PC crashing and hit 1000 PING. With the PC it was finally possible to play Minecraft on "fancy graphics". Which is just a lie that Mojang tell us as better than normal graphics.

It was, however, my first ever experience with building a PC. As a result I had stripped one of my front PC fans' screw hole. As a result, the fan is unbalanced. Every once in a while when I hardline the PC, the fan vibrates and oscillates. Creating an annoying br br br br br br.
